Who We Are

AgriNova is a digital platform that connects farmers, buyers, and agri-experts. Our mission is to empower rural communities through smart agriculture, innovation, and inclusive market access. Through our innovation wing, AgriNova Labs, we pioneer next-gen solutions for tomorrow’s farms.

🌱 Our Mission

To digitize farming practices and improve livelihoods through accessible technology.

🌍 Our Vision

To build a sustainable, tech-driven agricultural ecosystem across Africa and beyond.

Smart Agriculture Features

🧠 AI Crop Diagnostics

Farmers use mobile tools to scan leaves and detect diseases instantly with machine learning.

💧 Smart Irrigation

Sensor-based systems optimize water usage based on soil moisture and weather forecasts.

🚁 Drone Mapping

High-resolution aerial surveys help farmers monitor crop health and plan interventions.
